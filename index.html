Book your dream home at an affordable price at MRG Sector 106 in Gurgaon

As corporate employees and millennial continue to move to Gurgaon, the average prices of residential complexes show an upward trend. Well, you can still get a classy home in this city that comes within the affordable category. The MRG World Group has come up with one such project in Sector 106 in the city. MRG Sector 106, the new residential complex in the affordable category has been developed on the Dwarka Expressway. This strategic location of the estate ensures a seamless connectivity to the residents around the city. Moreover, this is the first project within the affordable category where the residents can enjoy a confirmed parking space.

MRG affordable Sector 106 has been developed over a massive space of 9,353 acres. Naturally, the residents can enjoy plenty of open and green spaces around the complex.
