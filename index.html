Check out MRG Skyline, the first affordable housing project in Gurgaon with confirmed parking space

Are you fancying the idea of purchasing an affordable home in Gurgaon? Well, the city promises a dynamic lifestyle, and most of the projects in Gurgaon come in the luxury category. However, here's MRG Skyline a residential project developed in Sector 106 of Gurgaon, where property owners can buy affordable homes.

These apartments come under the affordable housing sector, where you need to shell out just 1% GST. Besides, the prices of affordable properties in this project would come within your budget. The developers, MRG World, have priced these homes between INR 24.25 to 26.50 lakhs. One can choose from different configurations of 2 and 3 BHK apartments, available in this project.

What’s special about MRG Skyline?

Most of the property owner’s lookout for a dedicated car parking space in the complex where they purchase the apartment. However, when you check out affordable homes, it becomes difficult to find such a project. Although the affordable home developers integrate all the necessary features to ensure that the residents would remain comfortable, car parking space continues to be an issue.

However, at MRG World Skyline, the property owners can get a confirmed car parking space when they book the apartments. This implies that in case you own a vehicle, you need not look for a safe space to park it outside the complex.

Other specifications you might be interested to know

This is a massive residential project, developed across 9,353 acres. A lot of green and open spaces are available for the residents around these homes. The estate houses as many as 1,315 apartments, all of which come within the mentioned price range.

Therefore, the residents enjoy the freedom to choose their desired configuration from different available options. Regardless of the type of home you choose, you would enjoy the basic features and green spaces around.

The developers have also incorporated a strategic floor plan in this complex. This ensures that most of the common spaces would be available for the residents. With optimal security features and CCTV installations in the common areas, the residents here would remain safe and secure.

The proximity to Delhi and commercial areas

In the past, transportation happened to be an issue for residents living in Gurgaon. However, with the Dwarka Expressway coming up, this problem has been sorted out. The residents at MRG Skyline Sector 106 Gurgaon can avail of the seamless transportation system in the city to reach their commercial destinations and offices.

Even the airport and railways stations can be accessed quickly from these homes. For residents, it is crucial to ensure the availability of sophisticated education and healthcare facilities around the complex. Gurgaon doesn’t disappoint the property buyers in this respect too. A number of reputed schools, colleges and hospitals are located in close proximity to Skyline MRG affordable residential complex. Evidently, the residents would be at ease while navigating through the city when they move to this complex.

In case you want to book an apartment in this estate, this would be the right time to make the investment.
